The Fear is Real

If you're nervous, you're paying attention.

03

But Here's the Truth

AI does

  • Generate
  • Know patterns
  • Move fast
  • Scale infinitely

You do

  • Curate
  • Know context
  • Have taste
  • Build relationships

Taste is the last moat.

07

The Honest Assessment

What AI Does Well

  • Volume — 100 directions in an hour
  • Speed — first draft in seconds
  • Variations — infinite permutations
  • Exploration — "what if?" without cost

What AI Can't Do

  • Taste — knowing what's actually good
  • Context — why this client, this moment
  • Strategy — solving the real problem
  • Relationships — selling the idea
08

Where AI Fits in Your Process

Brief → [AI: Reference gathering] → Concept → [AI: Rapid exploration] → Direction → [AI: Variation generation] → Refinement → [Human: Final craft]

AI is your sketch pad, not your portfolio.

12

Prompting is a Skill

Bad: "Make me a cool ad" Good: "Editorial photography, single Toyota Camry, desert highway at golden hour, shot from low angle, dust particles in air, cinematic color grade, 35mm lens, no text"

Structure: Subject + Style + Environment + Technical + Constraints

13

The Art Director's Edge

  • You know what "good" looks like
  • You understand brand systems
  • You can art direct AI like a junior designer
  • You can sell the idea to stakeholders

AI doesn't have 10,000 hours. You do.

14

Ethical Guardrails

  • Don't claim AI work as hand-crafted
  • Don't train on others' work without consent
  • Disclose when appropriate (varies by client)
  • Copyright is still evolving — stay informed

The rules are being written. Help write them.
